About NEO GEN

Empowering India's youth with government internship opportunities

Our Mission

NEO GEN is India's premier government internship portal, connecting talented students with meaningful opportunities across various ministries, departments, and government agencies. We believe in building the future of public service through technology and innovation.

What We Offer

  • AI-Powered Matching: Our advanced algorithm matches your skills and interests with the perfect government internship opportunities.
  • ATS Score Optimization: Get detailed feedback on your resume to improve your chances of selection.
  • 24/7 AI Support: Our intelligent chatbot provides instant guidance and support throughout your journey.
  • Comprehensive Resources: Access guides, tutorials, and expert advice to excel in government internships.

Government Partnerships

We work closely with major government departments including NITI Aayog, Ministry of External Affairs, MeitY, ISRO, and many more to bring you authentic and valuable internship opportunities.

Accuracy of Information

  • Submitting forged, altered, or misleading documents is strictly prohibited.
  • We may verify information provided by you with issuing authorities and third-party KYC services.

Strict Policy on False Information

  • Providing false information, impersonation, or misrepresentation may result in any or all of the following actions without prior notice:
    • Immediate account suspension or permanent deletion.
    • Rejection or cancellation of applications and offers.
    • A ban from using the platform for up to 1 year or more, based on severity.
    • Notification to concerned Government departments/authorities.
    • Legal action as per applicable laws (including IT Act/IPC provisions).
  • Multiple offences will attract escalated penalties and permanent blacklisting.

Security, Monitoring and Compliance

  • Activities on this portal may be logged for security and audit purposes.
